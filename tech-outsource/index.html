FAQ.

  • 1. I’m still hesitant.

    Of course you are. That’s normal, and good. That’s why you have free consultation until all your doubts are settled. That’s why we follow global standards when conducting business, such as drafting internationally enforceable contracts to mitigate risk and ensure your safety. Because we want you to be excited for us, as we are for you.

  • 2. What, did you just say, free?

    Absolutely. You don’t pay for tech outsourcing consultations, no matter how long it takes. We know how difficult it is to find an employee, or a partner, who is truly talented and genuinely cares about your work. And we’re confident in our ability to deliver that.

  • 3. All I’m looking for is a way to save money.

    Aren’t we all? You can lower development costs up to 75% by outsourcing. And that’s not including the hours of saved sanity. And don’t even get us started on the diverse range of available tech.

    How is this possible? It’s just simple economics. Some countries have better talent pools in certain occupational fields than in others. This distorts supply. We cross borders to help you seize this opportunity. Sound good? We thought so, too.

  • 4. People say you shouldn’t outsource key tech.

    Completely true. Overseas project outsourcing isn't a one-size-fits-all magical genie. If you're creating cutting edge tech that’s easy to replicate, by all means, keep it secret and patent the hell out of it.

    But most entrepreneurs aren’t looking to clone dinosaurs or build laser swords. If you’re the average entrepreneur, it’s the idea that’s revolutionary, not the tech. And if you're the average entrepreneur, you statistically have only one shot to succeed. That’s why you want to boost marketing expenses and lower development costs. That’s where we come in.

  • 5. What’s your minimum project size?

    We don’t have one set in stone. Anything is possible if you throw enough money at it. We know that the art is in doing it faster and cheaper, which is something called ‘efficiency’, and we excel at it. But of course, we can’t do something that’s literally impossible, like building Amazon on a $100 budget. So give us a call, and we’ll try to figure out a way to help you.

  • 6. What about intellectual property?

    You get ownership of the software when the project ends. No hassle, no fuss.

  • 7. Can I get that in writing?

    Yes. We wouldn’t want it any other way. Your business needs to feel safe and secure, and we put contracts in place to help with that. It’s just smart practice.

  • 8. If you work with international firms, how do I pay?

    No need to worry about laws regulating international wire transfers, or the hassle of paying multiple global firms, because we take care of that for you. And to disperse risk, payments are made in increments, after each mutually agreed upon milestone is achieved. Oh, and no hidden fees or sneaky gimmicks.

  • 9. What if I don’t speak English, Polish, Hindi, or Russian?

    For many clients this is their number 1 concern with foreign outsourcing, so we’ve implemented a genius process to ensure that you get exactly what you’re looking for, regardless of the language you request it in. And of course we can always provide a translator if you require one.

  • 10. Okay, how do I start?

    We know you probably still have dozens of questions. And our friendly three-step introductory program is geared to answer all of them. Once you fill out the form below, our sales managers will contact you (normally within one business day), to schedule a meeting to discuss your project, and get you more acquainted with our services. We spare no care.
