When the going gets tough, it’s nice to have brilliant people on your side.

Our promises to our team are simple. We won’t hire assholes. We’ll do intellectually stimulating work. We’ll keep our vision socially responsible. Oh, and we won’t make promises we can’t keep. Sound good? Cool. You seem like our type of person.

We’ll Be Honest.

The work’s pretty demanding. But that’s okay because we truly enjoy our work.

Fit is of Utmost Importance.

We’re small and also slow at hiring. That’s because we want to work with people of the highest caliber. We’d rather work extra hours than hire someone who could hurt the integrity of our work. So we take steps to ensure a perfect fit.

Independent, but International.

We’re global and decentralized. Our teammates are located across the planet, and so we have no time for nonsense. We want people who don’t need supervision, who can adapt to other cultures, and who can perform above global standards. Most of all, we work at the perimeters of innovation, so we want people with the itch to explore fresh, uncharted territories.

Talent
Acquisition Process.

Simplicity is King

Send us a cover letter, resume, portfolio, or whatever it is you think is relevant, in whatever form you deem fitting. Then, we’ll either go grab coffee (if you’re in the neighborhood), or videochat. We don’t like paperwork any more than you do, so we keep it to a minimum.

Perpetual Hiring

At its core, a good company is just a group of good people dedicated to their work. We know first hand exactly how difficult it is for firms to find good people. And we are always hungry for good people. So we’re always hiring.

Composing Manual Benefit

We offer competitive pay as a rule, but we don’t have a linear salary structure, as different team members contribute in different ways. But we’re always open to new ideas, so tell us if you have a specific compensation plan in mind.
