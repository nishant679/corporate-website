We make tech and market opportunities accessible to everyone by breaking down borders. Through us companies expand their reach to the opposite side of the world, thereby tethering their growth to the rise of developing countries via transparent aggregation. And we're damn good at what we do.
We simplify services that were once only affordable to giant corporations. Overseas expansion has never been easier.

Introducing Border Tech.

We believe that businesses must use every resource possible in order to succeed in this increasingly competitive world. We help you utilize resources across the globe even from within the confines of your bedroom. Welcome to Border Tech.

Cross-Border
Software Development.

Tech is awesome. Tech fuels your business, creates a digitally-fluent force that helps your message resonate with customers, and most importantly, empowers the Davids to take on the Goliaths. That’s why we make tech affordable for everyone through cross-border arbitrage opportunities.

Cross-Border Market Entry Services.

Our belief is powerfully simple. If the average company in London will grow at 1.8% p.a., and the average company in Delhi will grow at 7% p.a., the average company in London can partake in the 7% growth rate if it can only cross borders with minimum transaction costs.
